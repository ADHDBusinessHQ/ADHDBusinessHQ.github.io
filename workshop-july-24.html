Is it my ADHD or my business?

A free online workshop for ADHD and neurodivergent entrepreneurs.

One hour that could change the way you look at what is not working.

You know something is not working.

You have the skills, the ideas, the experience. People who have worked with you can see how good you are at what you do.

But the business side keeps getting stuck. You plan and do not follow through. You write posts and do not publish them. You get interest and do not follow up. You start things and do not finish them. And every time it happens, you tell yourself the same thing: I should be able to do this by now.

The question you are really asking is whether the problem is you or whether the problem is how your business is set up. Because if it is you, that feels unfixable. But if it is the business, that is a different kind of problem entirely.

What we will do in this hour.

This is not a talk where I explain ADHD to you. You already live it. This is a working session where we look at the actual places your business is getting stuck and separate what is an ADHD pattern from what is a business problem, because the fix is different depending on which one it is, and most people are trying to fix the wrong thing.

  • Why the business advice that works for everyone else keeps falling apart for you, and what that actually means.
  • The difference between a motivation problem, an energy problem and a structure problem, and why it matters which one you are dealing with.
  • What "stop fixing the wrong thing" looks like in practice, using real patterns from real ADHD-run businesses.
  • One clear thing you can take away and do differently this week.

Who this is for.

ADHD and neurodivergent entrepreneurs who are good at what they do but find the business side harder than it feels like it should be. Diagnosed, self-identifying, questioning, AuDHD, or someone who recognises the patterns without needing a label.

If you have ever sat at your desk knowing exactly what you need to do and still not done it, and then felt worse about yourself because of it, this workshop is for you.

Who is running this.

I am Joanne Gibson, The ADHD Business Coach. I am AuDHD myself, which means I am not teaching this from a textbook. I have lived every pattern I coach people through.

I work with ADHD and neurodivergent entrepreneurs who are capable, experienced and good at their work but stuck in a business that was not built for the way their brain operates. I separate the ADHD pattern from the business problem so they stop trying to fix the wrong thing.
